*   Let your ring soak for 20-30 minutes.

*   Focus on the back of the diamond, where light can‚Äôt easily reach.
*   Rinse the ring thoroughly under warm running water.
*   Dry with a lint-free cloth.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions About Cleaning Diamond Rings

**Q: Can I use ultrasonic cleaners on my diamond ring?**
A: While ultrasonic cleaners can be effective, they're not always recommended. They can loosen stones in older or poorly set rings. If you choose to use one, ensure your ring is securely set and follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully.

**Q: How often should I clean my diamond ring?**
A: It depends on how often you wear it. A quick clean daily or every other day is ideal, with a deep clean every 1-2 weeks.

**Q: What if my ring has colored gemstones?**
A: Some gemstones are more delicate than diamonds. If your ring has colored stones, like a [sapphire and diamond ring](https://beacleaner.com/how-to-clean-a-sapphire-and-diamond-ring), consult a jeweler for specific cleaning instructions.

**Q: Can I use hand sanitizer to clean my diamond ring?**
A: No, avoid using hand sanitizer. It contains alcohol and other chemicals that can damage the metal setting and potentially dull the diamond.
