## üçã Tackling Water Spots: Vinegar to the Rescue üçã

Hard water can leave unsightly spots on your gold faucets. White vinegar is a natural and effective solution for dissolving these mineral deposits. However, it's important to dilute the vinegar to avoid damaging the finish. Always test in an inconspicuous area first!

*   Mix equal parts white vinegar and water in a spray bottle.
*   Spray the solution onto the affected areas of the faucet.
*   Let it sit for 5-10 minutes to allow the vinegar to break down the mineral deposits.
*   Gently scrub with a soft cloth or toothbrush.
